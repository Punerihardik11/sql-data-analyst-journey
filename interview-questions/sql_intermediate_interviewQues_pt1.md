🔥 TOPIC 1: SQL JOINs - Interview Questions
Practical Questions:
Amazon (Fresher): Write a query to find all customers and their orders, including customers who haven't placed any orders.
💡 Hint: LEFT JOIN
Deloitte: Find employees and their manager names from the same table (self-join scenario).
💡 Hint: JOIN table with itself
Myntra: Display product names with their category names. Some products don't have categories assigned.
💡 Hint: LEFT JOIN
EXL: Find all students and their course enrollments. Show students even if they're not enrolled in any course.
💡 Hint: LEFT JOIN
Accenture: Calculate total sales amount for each customer. Show customer name and total sales.
💡 Hint: JOIN + GROUP BY + SUM
Theory Questions:
Most Asked: What is the difference between INNER JOIN and LEFT JOIN?
Most Asked: What happens when you JOIN two tables without an ON clause?
Deloitte: Can you JOIN more than 2 tables? If yes, how?

🔀 TOPIC 2: UNION - Interview Questions
Practical Questions:
Myntra: Combine customer names from two different tables (VIP customers and regular customers) into one list.
💡 Hint: UNION
EXL: Create a single list showing all employee names from the 'IT' department and all manager names from the 'Management' department.
💡 Hint: UNION
Accenture: Merge product names from 'Electronics' category and 'Furniture' category into one column.
💡 Hint: UNION
Theory Questions:
Most Asked: What is the difference between UNION and UNION ALL?
Most Asked: What are the rules for using UNION? (Column count, data types)
Deloitte: Does UNION remove duplicates? How is it different from UNION ALL?

Practical Questions:
Amazon: Extract the domain name from email addresses (e.g., 'user@gmail.com' → 'gmail.com').
💡 Hint: SUBSTRING + LOCATE
Deloitte: Convert all employee names to uppercase and display their length.
💡 Hint: UPPER + LENGTH
Myntra: Remove leading and trailing spaces from product names before comparing them.
💡 Hint: TRIM
EXL: Find the position of '@' symbol in all email addresses.
💡 Hint: LOCATE
Accenture: Replace all occurrences of 'Mr.' with 'Mister' in employee names.
💡 Hint: REPLACE
Most Asked: Concatenate first name and last name with a space in between.
💡 Hint: CONCAT
Theory Questions:
Most Asked: What is the difference between CHAR_LENGTH and LENGTH?
Myntra: How do you extract a specific part of a string in SQL?
